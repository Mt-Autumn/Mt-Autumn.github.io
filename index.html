EdgeQuest

What is it?

EdgeQuest is our first game we are currently developing at Mt. Autumn. EdgeQuest is a roguelike inspired, top-down RPG... Until you take a closer look.

Real Time Combat

Combat is fast- and deadly. Even with the help of magic and weapons, only the smart survive.

Procedural World

The world is infinite and procedurally generated, with many handcrafted elements thrown in.

Manage Towns

Your help is needed. When you aren't busy dungeoneering, take a step back and help manage your local town.

Delve

This world holds secrets. Rumors tell of lost secrets far beneath the earth...

About Us

Who we are.

We are Mt. Autumn, a game design studio founded in Philadelphia. We believe in functional game design and approach challenges from a programatic point of view. We take influence from games new and old and hope to create an enjoyable gaming experience for players.

A Part of the EGS

We are proud members of Drexel University's Entreprenureal Game Studio where we recieve valuable guidance and share our experiences with fellow game designers.

Deeply Technical

We hope to create games that are not only fun to play but strive to also create something impressive from a technical perspective. We hope to utilize procedural generation for our upcoming game to push the game design forward.
